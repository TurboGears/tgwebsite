* Title: **TurboGears Lecture At Py-IL: Making A Flickr Clone**
* Author(s): Nadav Samet
* Date: 2007-02-20
* Subject: Part I: 1. Overview of TurboGears, 2. Project Philosophy, 3. Comparison with Other Web-Application Frameworks, 4. MVC Paradigm.
